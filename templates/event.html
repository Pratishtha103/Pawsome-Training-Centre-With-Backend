Events and Shows

Are you looking for a fun and educational way to spend your weekend with your furry friends? If so, you might want to check out the event and shows in our Pawsome training center happening in your City Park on following dates. You can learn from expert trainers how to teach your pets new tricks, improve their behavior, and bond with them. You can also watch amazing performances by talented animals and their owners, and even adopt new fur-bestiess!! Don't miss this opportunity to have a great time with your pets and meet other pet lovers. The event is free and open to all(you can donate to rescue centres as well), but registration is required. Visit our website for more details and sign up today!
