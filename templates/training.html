Pawsome Training Program

Basic Obedience Training

Basic obedience training focuses on teaching fundamental commands such as sit, stay, come, and leash manners. It helps establish communication between the owner and the dog, laying the groundwork for good behavior in various situations.

$50 - $100 per session

Puppy Socialization Classes

Puppy socialization classes are designed to introduce young puppies to different environments, people, and other dogs in a controlled and positive manner. This helps them develop confidence, learn appropriate social behaviors, and reduce fearfulness.

$20 - $40 per class

Behavior Modification

Behavior modification training addresses specific behavioral issues such as aggression, fearfulness, separation anxiety, or excessive barking. It involves identifying the underlying causes of the behavior and implementing training techniques to modify it.

$75 - $150 per session


Trick Training

Trick training focuses on teaching dogs fun and entertaining tricks beyond basic commands. It stimulates mental activity, builds confidence, and strengthens the bond between the owner and the dog.

$30 - $60 per session
